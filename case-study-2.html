Overview

The challenge

Doctor Anywhere started as a telehealth consultation app, but as the company expanded its services, the app became fragmented with multiple standalone features that didn't work together cohesively.

Users were confused about the full range of services available, leading to low discovery of new features and missed opportunities for cross-selling.

The solution

Unified home experience

Designed a new home screen that serves as a health dashboard, surfacing all services while prioritizing based on user context and history.

  • Personalized service recommendations
  • Quick actions for frequent tasks
  • Health insights and reminders
Home Screen Design

Connected service flows

Created seamless transitions between services. After a consultation, users can easily order prescribed medications, book follow-up tests, or file insurance claims.

  • Post-consultation action cards
  • One-tap prescription ordering
  • Integrated health records

Smart navigation system

Developed a modular navigation that adapts based on user type (individual vs. corporate) and most-used services.

  • Customizable bottom navigation

05

Key learnings

Progressive complexity

Super apps work best when they start simple and reveal complexity progressively as users explore and engage more deeply.

Connected, not cluttered

The key to super app success is smart connections between services, not cramming everything onto one screen.

Context is king

Showing the right service at the right time (like pharmacy after consultation) drives adoption better than generic promotions.
