Overview

What is the Soda HRA Portal?

A self-serve benefits platform where HR and SME owners create health and flex plans, assign employees, and launch coverage in minutes‚Äîno insurer-led manual setup. Employees access benefits, clinics, and claims in the same app.

The portal combines plan design (caps, co-pay, flex wallets), employee roster upload, and instant clinic access (620+ panel clinics; $13 GP consults) into a single flow, reducing admin time and giving companies budget clarity.
