How I use AI to design and ship faster

AI is embedded end-to-end: quick scans, playful protos, code-aware builds in Cursor, and fast sign-off. Not just designing—building and shipping faster.

01

Why AI stays in the loop

Faster learning loops

Goals → GPT scan → 2–3 options. No long research pause.

Feasibility first

Cursor keeps prototypes aligned to real APIs and components.

Human judgment

AI drafts; I decide. Constraints, clarity, empathy stay human.

Guardrails

  • Anchor on business + user goals
  • Source-check GPT outputs
  • Multiple variants before pick
  • Feasibility in Cursor/code
  • Document prompts and decisions
02

Workflow at a glance

1️⃣

Frame & constrain

Goals, KPIs, risks, and tech limits set the prompt scope.

2️⃣

Scan & seed concepts

GPT browsing for patterns and compliance; seed 2–3 options.

3️⃣

Prototype in Cursor

Build interactive variants, aligned to real APIs/components.

4️⃣

Validate & decide

Quick walkthroughs with users/eng; keep the winner, ship.

03

How I use GPT and Cursor

Market signals in minutes

GPT + browsing for regs, patterns, and pricing; outputs land as a one-pager to guide prototypes.

Feasibility inside code

Cursor searches repos for services, models, and components so prototypes stay shippable.

Variants and microcopy

Multiple flows, edge states, and localized copy; pick the clearest path before polish.

04

Practices in action

Storybook built with Cursor

Shipped a live Storybook; AI sped up scaffolding and docs.

Configurable care flows

Benchmarked care models, prototyped multiple onboarding and claims paths.

Data-aware prompts

Prompt recipes keep privacy and constraints so outputs stay compliant.
